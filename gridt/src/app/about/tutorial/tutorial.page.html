Hello, and welcome to the Gridt

Gridt is a signaling network that helps you reinforce actions that make our world a little nicer.

Users on the Gridt organize in movements and communicate with signals.

People in a movement act together on a specific goal, one action at a time.
This action can be anything: flossing, math homework, or a random act of kindness.

With the signal, you can indicate whether or not your have completed your action for the movement.

In a movement, you and your signal are only seen by your followers, while you see only your leaders and their signals.
Your job in the movement is to follow your leaders and to lead your followers in action.

Joining a movement will give you four leaders to follow. You may replace leaders whenever you activate your signal.

In a movement, you can attach a message to your signal to encourage your followers.

You are free to join and leave any movement at any time.

Use Gridt to support all actions and movements that will shape our future planet and communities. We encourage you to interact with other users and our project outside the app and to start your own movements!
